    Monastero Santa Rosa Hotel & Spa
    Salerno, Italy
    The hotel features a Michelin-starred restaurant, Il Refettorio, which offers a menu of contemporary Italian cuisine prepared with fresh, local ingredients. The hotel also has a bar and a wine cellar featuring a selection of local and international wines.
    Grand Hotel Tremezzo
    Lake Como, Italy
    The Grand Hotel Tremezzo features 92 guest rooms and suites, each decorated in a classic Italian style with elegant furnishings and luxurious fabrics. Some of the rooms offer stunning views of Lake Como and the surrounding mountains.
    The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur
    Udaipur, India
    It is situated on the banks of Lake Pichola and is spread across 50 acres of landscaped gardens. The hotel is designed in the style of a traditional Rajasthani palace and features intricate domes, arches, and fountains. The hotel offers 87 rooms.
    AKA Beverly Hills
    Los Angeles, United States
    The suites at AKA Beverly Hills range in size from one to two bedrooms, and each features contemporary furnishings, high-quality linens, and designer bath products. The kitchens are fully equipped with top-of-the-line appliances and everything guests need to prepare their own meals, including cookware, dishes, and utensils.
